Meet Your Heroes

Henry Uys

Jari Salminen
A relentless innovator, Henry Uys (pronounced "ace") has been creating and making for the past ten years. Cutting his teeth on 3D printing a CNC machine from scratch, this genuine Afrikaaner has come by his mechanical genius honestly. That is to say, he hasn't given up yet. After learning to walk, Jari Salminen's parents put him to work. Tiny hammer in one crisp paw, petit saw in the opposite, this child of the woods stopped at nothing to improve his craft. Still at it, he has decided to bring the public into the creative process.
